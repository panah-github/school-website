campus life

school stage

the thing you first see when you enter the school

The stage is the place where the choir sings the assembly songs everyday and all of the award giving ceremonies are held here. All the dances and functions happen on the stage and the spectators sit on the chairs in the ground in front of the stage.

school auditorium

this auditorium is a part of the middle school

All the functions that happen in the primary wing school happen in the auditorium. The primary wing school is like 5-6 kms away from the high school and this school so much more beautiful than our high school.

kg playground

this is where the kg children play

This playground is in the kg school which is next to the primary wing school and children have 2-3 games periods per week. They come with their class teachers here or they go to the play room which has lots of toys to play.

school achievements

50th anniversary of SHS Moga
completed the golden jubilee on 5 april,2021

During this event all the teachers visited the school and a food fest was conducted for them and the pictures were clicked. The teachers prepared different dances in the auditorium and made a video about it and sent it to the children coz due to covid-19 children weren't allowed to come to school.
